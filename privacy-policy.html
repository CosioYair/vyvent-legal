Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 13, 2026  |  Last Updated: April 13, 2026

Vyvent ("we," "us," or "our") operates the Vyvent mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our App. Please read this policy carefully. By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your name, email address, and profile photo. You may also sign in using Google OAuth, in which case we receive your name, email, and profile picture from Google.
  • Profile Information: Bio, display name, and avatar image you choose to provide.
  • Event Content: Event titles, descriptions, dates, locations, and photos you upload when creating or interacting with events.
  • Messages: Content of messages you send through the App's messaging feature.
  • Stories: Photos and media you share as stories.

1.2 Information Collected Automatically

  • Location Data: With your permission, we collect your precise location to show nearby events and enable location-based features. You can revoke this permission at any time through your device settings.
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system version, and unique device identifiers for push notification delivery.
  • Push Notification Tokens: We collect Expo push tokens to send you notifications about events, messages, and other App activity.
  • Usage Data: Features you use, events you view, and interactions within the App (e.g., attendance, comments, follows).

1.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Google Sign-In: If you authenticate via Google, we receive your Google account email, name, and profile picture. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or other Google services.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With Other Users: Your profile information, events you create, stories, and comments are visible to other App users as part of the App's social features. Messages are only visible to the participants of the conversation.
  • Service Providers: We use Supabase (database and authentication), Google Cloud (maps and sign-in), and Expo (push notifications and updates). These providers process data on our behalf under contractual obligations.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Vyvent, our users, or the public.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • Your data is stored in Supabase-managed PostgreSQL databases with Row Level Security (RLS) enabled on all tables.
  • All data is transmitted over HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • Authentication tokens are stored securely on-device using Expo SecureStore (hardware-backed keychain/keystore).
  • Photos are stored in Supabase Storage with access controls appropriate to the content type (public for event photos and avatars, private with signed URLs for stories).

While we implement industry-standard security measures, no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • Event data persists for the lifetime of the event and may remain visible to attendees afterward.
  • Messages are retained for the duration of the conversation.
  • Stories may expire based on your subscription plan settings.
  • If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 All Users

  • Access & Correction: You can view and update your profile information at any time through the App.
  • Delete Account: You may request account deletion by contacting us. We will process your request within 30 days.
  • Location: You can disable location access through your device settings at any time.
  • Push Notifications: You can disable push notifications through your device settings.
  • Camera & Photos: You can revoke camera and photo library access through your device settings.

6.2 European Economic Area (EEA) Residents — GDPR

If you are in the EEA, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation:

  • Right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data
  • Right to restrict or object to processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to withdraw consent at any time
  • Right to lodge a complaint with a supervisory authority

Our legal basis for processing: consent (location, notifications), contract performance (account, messaging), and legitimate interests (security, fraud prevention).

6.3 California Residents — CCPA

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information is collected, used, and shared
  • Request deletion of personal information
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13 (or 16 in the EEA). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we learn that we have collected data from a child under the applicable age, we will delete it promptly. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review their privacy policies.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of material changes by posting the updated policy in the App or sending a push notification. Your continued use of the App after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
